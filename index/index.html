Welcome to TDSA!

This site is the home of Len Baxley, Founder and Originator of TDSA shooting.

If you want to learn from the very best, you have come to the right place!

TDSA is much more than a typical gun range. This is a one stop shop for shooting expertise. We offer the training, workspace, and competition for anyone to walk in as a beginner and leave as a champion shooter.

We are Texas Defensive Shooting Academy, the premier firearms and tactics training site in North Texas. Located Southeast of Dallas off of Highway 45 just outside of Ferris, TDSA is a 25 acre outdoor training facility that boasts plenty of secluded, private training space including:

  • 11 tactical bays (25 yd range)
    • You may shoot pistols, shotguns, and rifles in tactical bays.
    • Rapid fire, draw from holster, transition from rifle to pistol, running while shooting, etc. are all permitted.
    • Steel in tactical bays must be shot with pistol calibers .45 ACP or smaller or using shotguns with birdshot or target load ammunition. Shooting of steel with rifles is NOT ALLOWED.
    • (NOT ON DUELING TREES, SUSPENDED STEEL, PLATE RACK, OR ANY IN ANY BAY MARKED ".22 ONLY")
    • 22 long rifle only bay includes specialty steel for .22 LR ONLY.
  • 300 yd rifle range
      • target backers at 25, 50, 100, 200, and 300 yds
    • bench-rest and prone only
    • NO PISTOLS IN RIFLE RANGE
    • YOU MUST BE QUALIFIED BY RANGE STAFF TO SHOOT IN RIFLE RANGE.
  • 400 yd rifle range (you must be qualified by range staff)
  • 3 gun and training area (competition and range staff training sessions only)
    • Come out for training or competition and see our training and 3 gun areas including:
    • the street
    • the 360 range
